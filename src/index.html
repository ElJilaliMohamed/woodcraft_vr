Become a master of woodwork
without a single splinter

Designed for those in vocational and technical education to learn through VR,
mastering panel saws and milling machines in a safe, interactive environment.
Learn efficiently as you immerse yourself in realistic scenarios, turning learning into a game-like experience. Mistakes become valuable lessons without any risk of injury, fostering skill development effectively.

The panel saw

  • naming and understanding the components and operation of the machine
